TeamLab is live no matter what!

April 22, 2011

Greetings to all!

The end of this week presented an unpleasant surprize- TeamLab temporary downtime that was caused by the unexpected server issues of Amazon Web Services. You may have experienced inconvenience due to this unforeseen problem. Please accept our sincere apologies!

Currently TeamLab is operating properly. Our specialists have taken all possible measures to restore the portal with the shortest delay. While some Amazon servers are still disrupted, you can already use your corporate portals. Be sure – your information is safe as all the data has been successfully recovered in time. If you have any questions- feel free to contact us via support@teamlab.com.

Take a rest and have a nice weekend!

P.S. In case you haven’t noticed yet, TeamLab website has a new look. We have refreshed the interface design, optimized the menu, added new useful content – all done for you to get more pleasure while working on TeamLab

What’s next, TeamLab?

April 14, 2011

This month we have received several requests from our users asking to post about TeamLab future enhancements. We couldn’t but follow your wish – today we publish the list of the upcoming new features and solutions.

TeamLab mobile application is the main on the agenda. The first light version, optimized for iPhone, iPod and Android, will appear in May and include Community module: you will be able to view and search through employees list, read and comment blogs, forums, events, bookmarks. Project Management module will come next allowing you to do the following activities: add, edit and close tasks, take part in “Discussions”, view “My team” info, keep up-to-date with “My projects” newsfeed, send e-mail to team members. While iPad users will be able to enter and work at the general portal.
